Three ways the loop can end

What plateau means. Λ keeps getting updated but barely moves — 8.0 → 8.1 → 8.2. The documents you are still reading are not telling you anything you did not already know. Reading more will not change the answer, so stop and decide on what is in hand.

Plateau is not a boundary. Nothing was crossed. It is a judgment that further reading is a waste, and it needs two numbers written down: how small a change counts as flat, and over how many updates. Without those it is not a rule, it is a feeling.

Also log which exit fired on every case. If most cases end on plateau rather than on a boundary, the system in production is not the system the poster describes.
